--- title: New paper in Bioelectronic Medicine date: 2025-02-14 --- Congratulations to Sachin Salim for his paper acceptance in the Bioelectronic Medicine journal, titled "In vivo electrophysiology recordings and computational modeling can predict octopus arm movement". You can read it [here](https://bioelecmed.biomedcentral.com/articles/10.1186/s42234-025-00166-9).

Sachin is currently pursuing his PhD in Electrical & Computer Engineering at USC where he is developing a deep learning framework using quantile regression to synthesize missing MRI contrasts.

Congrats, Sachin!
