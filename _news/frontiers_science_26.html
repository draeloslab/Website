--- title: Anne becomes a 2026 Frontiers of Science Fellow date: 2026-2-25 --- Anne was named a [2026 Frontiers of Science Fellow](https://www.nasonline.org/news/national-academy-of-sciences-selects-the-2026-frontiers-of-science-fellows/) by the National Academy of Sciences. She is one of 81 scientists receiving this fellowship this year.
