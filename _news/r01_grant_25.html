--- title: Draelos Lab is awarded NIH R01 Funding date: 2025-08-01 image: /img/nih-r01.png image_pos: center --- The Draelos Lab was awarded an NIH RO1 Grant for the project "Multimodal Probabilistic Machine Learning for Uncovering Latent Factors of Resilience in Alzheimer's Disease", in collaboration with the [Kaczorowski Lab](https://kaczorowski.lab.medicine.umich.edu/)

This is funded by the National Institute On Aging of the National Institutes of Health under Award Number R01AG092459.
