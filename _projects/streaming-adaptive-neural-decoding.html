--- title: "Steaming Adaptive Neural Decoding" image: /img/streaming-neural-decoding.png active: true order: 3 --- To better model temporal patterns in neural activity as it relates to ongoing motor behavior, we develop streaming algorithms through a probabilistic sequence decoding framework. Our goal is to obtain latent dynamical models that can flexibly adapt to ongoing distributional shifts. Through this framework, we aim to improve the stability or adaptability of BCI decoders across time and task, thus reducing or eliminating the need for frequent re-calibration.
Figure: Simulated rotational drift in [MC Maze](https://neurallatents.github.io/datasets.html) experimental neural data. Performance of neural decoders goes down as a function of drift. Our algorithm PSD maintains performance by adapting to the changed neural dynamics.
